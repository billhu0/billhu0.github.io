Decomposition of Even and Odd

In general, aka_kak​ is complex, therefore this is not read and imaginary decomposition. However, this is the even and odd decomposition.

不是实部和虚部的分解，而是偶函数与奇函数的分解。

x(t)=∑k=−∞∞akejkω0t=∑k=−∞∞(akcos⁡(kω0t)+jaksin⁡(kω0t))=a0+∑k>0((ak+a−k)cos⁡(kω0t)+j(ak−a−k)sin⁡(kω0t))\begin{aligned} x(t) &= \sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} a_k e^{jk\omega_0 t}\\ &= \sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} (a_k \cos(k\omega_0 t) + j a_k \sin(k\omega_0 t))\\ &= a_0 + \sum_{k>0} ((a_k + a_{-k}) \cos(k\omega_0 t) + j (a_k - a_{-k}) \sin(k \omega_0 t)) \end{aligned} x(t)​=k=−∞∑∞​ak​ejkω0​t=k=−∞∑∞​(ak​cos(kω0​t)+jak​sin(kω0​t))=a0​+k>0∑​((ak​+a−k​)cos(kω0​t)+j(ak​−a−k​)sin(kω0​t))​

x(t)x(t)x(t) even →\rightarrow→ all sine terms vanish →\rightarrow→ ak=a−ka_k = a_{-k}ak​=a−k​

x(t)x(t)x(t) odd →\rightarrow→ all cosine terms vanish →\rightarrow→ ak=−a−ka_k = -a_{-k}ak​=−a−k​
